HACK@10

What is Hack@10?

Hack@10 is a hackathon-style competition that commenced its journey in 2012 when it was first hosted at UNITEN, continued in 2018 and 2021, and is set to return this year in 2023. It is organized through the collaboration of two clubs: CLICQ Club and Pro-C Club, with the primary objective of providing participants

with practical exposure to real-world challenges, enabling them to tackle authentic problems and refine their problem-solving skills, with a focus on exploring problem-solving through the application of artificial intelligence (AI) in 2023.

FAQ

What is Hack@10?

Hack@10 is an annual hackathon that started in 2012 and is set to return in 2023. It's a collaboration between CLICQ Club and Pro-C Club, offering participants practical experience in tackling real-world challenges and enhancing their problem-solving skills. In 2023, the focus is on problem-solving with artificial intelligence (AI).

How can I participate?

You can participate by registering through the provided registration form on the website. To do so, simply fill up the registration form at Registration form on our website. Follow the registration instructions, provide the required information and make sure to join our discord server

Is it only for Malaysians?

Nope, this competition is open to participants of all nationalities.

Can my team have members from different universities?

Yes, your team can include members from different universities.

I'm not physically in Malaysia. Can I still participate?

No, we made sure to provide full virtual support for the event so that we can give a chance to everyone in the world. So whether you're in malaysia or outside, you're free to join. hough, do note that the interntaional spots are limited.

Is there a registration fee?

No! participation is totally free!

Can I participate as an individual, or do I need to be in a team?

You can participate individually or in a team. A team should have at least two members, with the option to include a maximum of three members. Of these, at least one member should be a coder.

When and where is the hackathon taking place?

The hackathon consists of two rounds: the virtual preliminary round scheduled from November 27th to December 3rd, 2023, and the hybrid final round on December 16th and 17th, 2023, which includes both virtual and physical participation. The final round for physical attendees will take place at UNITEN, Malaysia.
