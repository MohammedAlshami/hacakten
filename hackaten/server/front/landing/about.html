What is Hack@10?

Hack@10 is a hackathon-style competition that commenced its journey in 2012 when it was first hosted at UNITEN, continued in 2018 and 2021, and is set to return this year in 2023. It is organized through the collaboration of two clubs: CLICQ Club and Pro-C Club, with the primary objective of providing participants with practical exposure to real-world challenges, enabling them to tackle authentic problems and refine their problem-solving skills, with a focus on exploring problem-solving through the application of artificial intelligence (AI) in 2023.
